𐤀 About the Project

Project "Golem" is not a religion. It is a restoration.

What is "Golem"?

"Golem" (גֹּלֶם) is unformed matter, raw clay from which the Creator shapes form. So is our project: we take the raw text, distorted by centuries of translation, and return it to its original form — in the Hebrew language in which every word was given.

The TaNaKh is not the Bible. The Bible is a product of Hellenization, Roman censorship, and Slavic adaptation. Project "Golem" strips away layers of translational distortion to reveal the original as it was meant to be.

Why does this matter?

The system replaced the personal Name Yahveh (יהוה) with "Lord" — a title borrowed from Greek and Roman hierarchy. It turned "mashiach" (anointed one — a function) into "Christ" (a title, then a name). It made "torah" (instruction) into "law", and "teshuvah" (return) into "repentance".

Each such distortion is not an accident. It is architecture. The system deliberately translated the TaNaKh to serve empires, not Truth.

What do we do?

We restore. We take every key word of the TaNaKh and show its root, its meaning in Hebrew, and how it was distorted in translations. We do not teach "belief" — we teach reading, analyzing, seeing.

We expose systems that present themselves as "the natural order of things": sports as religion, medicine as priesthood, education as training, money as idol. All of these are elilim (nothings) that people worship instead of Yahveh.

This is not a religion

We have no dogmas, no cult, no "salvation through membership". We do not call you to join an organization or pay tithes. We do not claim ultimate truth — we only show where the original says one thing and translations say another. The choice is always yours.

Yahveh is one. Torah is instruction. Mashiach is anointed. Teshuvah is return. This is not a creed. These are facts of the text. Read the original and judge for yourself.
